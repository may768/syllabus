Chemical Technology (Organic) Lab

CHEMICAL TECHNOLOGY (ORGANIC) LAB
Paper Code: PCC 156
Credits: 1.5   |   Maximum Marks: 50

Course Outcomes

  • Determination of Acid value
  • Determination of Iodine value
  • Determination of Saponification value
  • Determination of Reducing and Non-reducing sugars by:
    • (i) Fehling’s method
    • (ii) Pavy’s method
  • Determination of Free and combined alkali, Total fatty matter, and Moisture

List of Practicals

  1. Oils and Fats: Determination of acid value, iodine value, and saponification value.
  2. Carbohydrates: Determination of reducing and non-reducing sugars by:
    • (i) Fehling’s method
    • (ii) Pavy’s method
  3. Soaps: Determination of free and combined alkali, total fatty matter, moisture, and insoluble matter.
