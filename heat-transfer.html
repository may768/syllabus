Heat Transfer

HEAT TRANSFER
Course Code: PCC105

Unit I – Conduction

  • Steady state conduction in one dimensional system
  • General conduction equation
  • Effect of variable thermal conductivity
  • Steady state conduction involving internal heat generation
  • Lagging on pipes
  • Critical thickness of insulation on pipes
  • Extended surfaces of uniform thickness
  • Fin effectiveness and fin efficiency

Unit II – Convection and Radiation

  • Convection
    • Free and forced convection
    • Concept of heat transfer co-efficient
    • Dimensionless numbers in free and forced convection
    • Dimensional analysis
    • Experimental determination of heat transfer coefficient
    • Common working correlations
  • Radiation Heat Transfer
    • Black body radiation and gray body radiation
    • Physical mechanism of radiation
    • Radiation properties and shape factor
    • Heat exchange between non-black bodies
    • Radiation shields
    • Pyrometry and effect of radiation on temperature measurement

Unit III – Condensation, Boiling and Evaporation

  • Condensation and Boiling
    • Condensation heat transfer phenomenon
    • Film condensation on vertical plates and cylinders
    • Condensation on horizontal cylinders
    • Effect of non-condensable gases and vapor velocity on condensation
    • Pool boiling
    • Forced convection boiling
    • Working correlations for pool boiling
  • Evaporation
    • Types of evaporators
    • Single and multiple effect evaporators
    • Single and multiple-effect calculations
    • Evaporator capacity and economy
    • Effect of liquid head and boiling point elevation
    • Methods of feeding

Unit IV – Heat Exchangers

  • Various types of heat exchangers
  • Overall heat transfer coefficients
  • Heat exchanger mean temperature differences
  • Heat exchanger effectiveness
  • Number of transfer units (NTU method)
